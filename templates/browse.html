This chart intends on answering the question of the average happiness ratings of each borough. I chose to plot this on a mapbox plot through plotly express. This was chosen as the dataset contains geographical location in the form of a longitude and latitude of each borough. The mapbox plot allows the plotting of the happiness data alongside the actual location and this is displayed to the user. It also allows a colour scale which I used to display the difference each borough is from the UK average value. A lighter colour indicates that the sampled populace of a borough is on average found to be generally happier about the area they live in and vice versa for darker colours. It is also interactive and the user can zoom in to gain a relative reference of the mapping of different boroughs. The map is also based on openstreet maps.
